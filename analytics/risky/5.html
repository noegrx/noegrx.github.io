The efficient frontier - Risky

On the graph

Optimal portfolios are seen on the efficient frontier. The portfolios located below the efficient border are not optimal because they do not offer a sufficient return for the level of risk.

In this graphs, we can see that a lot of points are below 0 for the returns, which means that this portfolio have a negative returns.

The volatility is higher than the safe portfolio but it is logical because the more you take risks, the more your portfolio is volatile.
