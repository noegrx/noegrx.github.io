L'Oréal - Safy

On the graph

The static graph represents L’Oréal’s share price in dollars over one year.

The graph shows several important fluctuations in the price but it remains above $230 for almost six months. Indeed, the price reached its highest level in January 2020 with $275 but fell to $250 a few days later.

Rising above $270 at the end of February, the price fell sharply with the start of the coronavirus crisis to $205, a drop of $65. The fall in stocks was a widespread phenomenon on the stock markets and L'Oréal was not spared, in particular due to its activity sector located in cosmetics.

Conclusion

Finally, the expected rebound was faster. In April 2020, the share price rose $50 to reach $255. More recenlty, in the beginning of May 2020, the share price was $265, which is rather a high level compared to the price level over one year.
